Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Humanity Ledger / Whale Alert Network. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information. Our core philosophy is the Doctrine of Cryptographic Anonymity: we do not store, persist, or correlate your cryptographic keys.

2. Data Collection and Usage

We do not require, request, or possess mechanisms to store your Personal Identifiable Information (PII) such as legal names, residential coordinates, or traditional financial banking records. Your identity on our platform is cryptographic.

  • Local Execution: All cryptographic primitives, including private keys, are executed entirely within your local environment.
  • Analytics: We use strictly anonymous, aggregate telemetry to monitor node health and RPC latency. IP addresses are hashed using a one-way cryptographic salt for rate-limiting purposes to protect against DDoS attacks.

3. Cookies and Local Storage

We use local storage and secure, encrypted cookies (such as the sovereign handshake) strictly for session management and securing your terminal state. We do not use third-party tracking cookies.

4. Public Blockchain Data

Please note that any transaction executed through our terminal interacts with public blockchains. We cannot delete or obscure your on-chain history, as the public ledger is immutable and visible to third parties.

5. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact our support channels via Telegram or our official website.
