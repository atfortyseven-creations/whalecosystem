## 3. COST STRUCTURE & UNIT ECONOMICS

The Zero-Trust Architecture is designed for maximum capital efficiency. We utilize Edge computing (Vercel) and serverless databases (Supabase/Prisma) to maintain a highly variable cost structure that scales linearly with revenue.

### 3.1 COGS (Cost of Goods Sold)
- **RPC Providers (Infura/Alchemy/GetBlock):** ~10% of revenue. Fixed requests are minimized via the local Memory Matrix cache.
- **Graph Database (Neo4j AuraDS):** ~5% of revenue.
- **Edge Compute & Telemetry (Vercel/PostgreSQL):** ~3% of revenue.
- **Gross Margin:** ~82% (Excellent SaaS margin).

### 3.2 Operating Expenses (OPEX)
- **R&D (Engineering & Cryptography):** 55% of OPEX. Continual enhancement of the Z-Score algorithm and ZK-SNARK identity proofs.
- **Sales & Marketing:** 25% of OPEX. Focus on B2B direct outreach and technical content (Whitepapers).
- **Compliance & Legal:** 20% of OPEX. Maintaining eIDAS 2.0 alignment, smart contract audits (Hacken/Nethermind), and data privacy (GDPR) enforcement.
